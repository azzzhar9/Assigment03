Leave of Absence Policy

Types of Leave:

1. Vacation Leave:
- Request vacation at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal
- Manager approval required for all vacation requests
- Blackout periods may apply during peak business seasons
- Vacation cannot be taken during the first 90 days of employment

2. Sick Leave:
- No advance notice required for sick leave
- Notify your manager as soon as possible if you'll be absent
- Medical documentation required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave is separate from vacation leave

3. Personal Leave:
- Up to 3 days of unpaid personal leave per year
- Requires manager and HR approval
- Can be used for personal matters not covered by other leave types

4. Bereavement Leave:
- 3 days of paid bereavement leave for immediate family members
- 1 day for extended family members
- Immediate family includes: spouse, children, parents, siblings

5. Jury Duty:
- Paid time off for jury duty service
- Provide jury duty summons to HR
- Any jury duty pay received must be reported to HR

6. Military Leave:
- Unpaid leave for military service obligations
- Job protection guaranteed under USERRA
- Contact HR for military leave requests

Leave Request Process:
1. Submit request through HR portal
2. Manager reviews and approves/denies
3. HR confirms and updates records
4. Calendar is updated automatically

Emergency Leave:
- For unexpected emergencies, contact your manager and HR immediately
- Documentation may be required depending on circumstances
- HR will work with you to determine appropriate leave type

Extended Leave:
Extended leave beyond standard vacation, sick, or personal leave requires special approval and planning.

FMLA Leave (Family Medical Leave Act):
Employees who have worked for the company for at least 12 months and have worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months are eligible for up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave per year for:
- Birth and care of newborn child
- Adoption or foster care placement
- Serious health condition of employee
- Caring for spouse, child, or parent with serious health condition
- Qualifying military exigency

To request FMLA leave:
1. Notify HR at least 30 days in advance when possible
2. Complete FMLA request form
3. Provide medical certification from healthcare provider
4. HR will determine eligibility and approve/deny request
5. Benefits continue during FMLA leave
6. Job restoration guaranteed upon return

Medical Leave:
For serious medical conditions not covered by FMLA or exceeding FMLA duration:
- Request must include medical documentation
- HR and management review on case-by-case basis
- May be paid or unpaid depending on circumstances
- Disability insurance may apply for extended absences
- Return to work may require medical clearance

Parental Leave:
The company provides paid parental leave for the birth or adoption of a child:
- Primary caregiver: 12 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Can be combined with FMLA for extended time
- Benefits continue during parental leave
- Requires 30-day advance notice when possible

Sabbatical Leave:
Employees with 10+ years of service may be eligible for sabbatical:
- Up to 3 months unpaid leave
- Requires business unit approval
- Must provide 6 months advance notice
- Used for professional development, personal projects, or rest
- Job restoration not guaranteed but company will make best effort
- Benefits may be continued at company's discretion

Leave Without Pay:
In certain circumstances, unpaid leave may be granted:
- Personal emergencies or hardships
- Educational opportunities
- Extended travel
- Requires manager and HR approval
- Maximum duration typically 30 days
- Benefits continuation at employee expense
- No guarantee of job restoration

Leave Accrual and Carryover:

Vacation Accrual:
- 0-2 years: 10 days per year
- 3-5 years: 15 days per year
- 6-10 years: 20 days per year
- 11+ years: 25 days per year
- Accrues monthly at 1/12 of annual amount
- Maximum carryover: 1.5x annual accrual
- Unused vacation over cap forfeited at year end

Sick Leave Accrual:
- All employees: 10 days per year
- Accrues monthly at 0.833 days
- Maximum accrual: 60 days
- Cannot be cashed out upon termination

Holidays:
The company observes 11 paid holidays including New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and others.
