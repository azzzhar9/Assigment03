Compensation and Salary Policy

Salary Structure:
Our company uses a competitive salary structure based on market rates, experience, and performance. Salaries are reviewed annually during the performance review cycle.

Salary Components:
- Base Salary: Fixed annual compensation
- Performance Bonus: Up to 20% of base salary based on individual and company performance
- Equity: Stock options available for eligible positions
- Sign-on Bonus: May be offered for certain positions (typically $5,000-$10,000)

Salary Ranges:
- Entry Level: $50,000 - $70,000
- Mid Level: $70,000 - $100,000
- Senior Level: $100,000 - $150,000
- Executive Level: $150,000+

Salary Review Process:
1. Annual reviews conducted in Q1
2. Market analysis performed by HR
3. Performance evaluation considered
4. Manager recommendations reviewed
5. Final approval by department head and HR
6. Effective date: April 1st

Promotion Increases:
- Internal promotions typically include 10-15% salary increase
- Additional equity may be granted
- New title and responsibilities reflected in compensation

Cost of Living Adjustments:
- Reviewed annually based on inflation and market conditions
- Applied uniformly across all employees
- Separate from performance-based increases

Salary Confidentiality:
- Salaries are confidential information
- Discussion of salaries is protected under labor law
- HR maintains strict confidentiality of compensation data

Compensation Philosophy:
- Pay for performance
- Market competitive
- Internal equity maintained
- Transparent review process

Bonus Structure:
- Annual bonus based on company and individual performance
- Payout in Q1 of following year
- Eligibility requires employment through December 31st
- Prorated for partial year employees

Retention Bonuses:
- May be offered for critical roles
- Typically 15-25% of annual salary
- Paid in installments over 12-24 months
- Requires continued employment

Stock Options:
- Granted to eligible employees
- 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
- Exercise period of 10 years
- Subject to company performance and market conditions

Compensation Disputes:
- Contact HR for compensation questions
- Formal review process available
- Market data provided upon request
- Appeals process for salary decisions
