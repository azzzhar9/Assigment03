Employee Benefits Policy

Our company offers comprehensive benefits to all full-time employees. The benefits package includes:

Health Insurance:
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment
- Company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 60% for dependents
- Annual deductible of $500 for individuals, $1000 for families

Retirement Benefits:
- 401(k) plan with company matching up to 6% of salary
- Vesting period is 3 years
- Company matches 50% of employee contributions

Paid Time Off:
- 15 days of paid vacation per year for employees with less than 5 years of service
- 20 days for employees with 5-10 years of service
- 25 days for employees with more than 10 years of service
- Vacation days accrue monthly and can be carried over up to 5 days per year

Sick Leave:
- 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Sick leave does not accrue and resets annually on January 1st
- Unused sick leave cannot be carried over

Parental Leave:
- 12 weeks of paid parental leave for new parents (birth or adoption)
- Available to all employees regardless of gender
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth or adoption

Wellness Program:
- Annual wellness stipend of $500
- Gym membership reimbursement up to $50/month
- Mental health support through Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Additional Benefits:

Life Insurance:
- Company-provided life insurance equal to 2x annual salary
- Additional coverage available for purchase
- Beneficiary designation required
- Coverage effective on first day of employment

Disability Insurance:
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 26 weeks
- Company-paid premiums
- Coverage begins after 90 days of employment

Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA):
- Healthcare FSA: Up to $3,050 per year
- Dependent Care FSA: Up to $5,000 per year
- Pre-tax contributions
- Use-it-or-lose-it policy applies

Health Savings Account (HSA):
- Available with high-deductible health plans
- Company contribution of $500 per year
- Triple tax advantage
- Funds roll over year to year

Commuter Benefits:
- Pre-tax transit passes
- Parking reimbursement
- Bike commuting support
- Carpool incentives

Employee Discounts:
- Corporate discounts on various services
- Technology purchase programs
- Travel and entertainment discounts
- Local business partnerships

Work-Life Balance:
- Flexible work arrangements
- Remote work options
- Compressed work weeks
- Part-time opportunities
- Job sharing programs

Education Benefits:
- Tuition reimbursement up to $5,000/year
- Professional development budget
- Certification support
- Conference attendance
- Learning management system access

Recognition Programs:
- Employee of the month
- Years of service recognition
- Peer recognition platform
- Spot bonuses
- Achievement awards

Additional Perks:
- Free snacks and beverages
- Company events and outings
- Volunteer time off (16 hours/year)
- Sabbatical program (after 5 years)
- Pet-friendly office (where applicable)
