Employee Onboarding Process

Welcome to the company! Our onboarding process is designed to help you succeed from day one.

Pre-Boarding (Before Start Date):
- Complete background check and drug screening
- Submit required documentation (I-9, W-4, direct deposit form)
- Review employee handbook
- Set up company email and accounts
- Schedule first day orientation

Day One:
- Welcome meeting with HR (9:00 AM)
- Office tour and workspace setup
- IT equipment and access provisioning
- Introduction to team members
- Review of company policies and culture

Week One:
- Complete mandatory training modules:
  * Code of Conduct
  * Information Security
  * Workplace Safety
  * Diversity and Inclusion
- Meet with manager to discuss role expectations
- Set up development environment and tools
- Attend department meeting

First Month:
- Complete all required certifications
- Shadow team members to learn processes
- Attend company all-hands meeting
- Schedule 30-day check-in with manager
- Begin working on initial projects

Onboarding Checklist:
- [ ] Background check completed
- [ ] All paperwork submitted
- [ ] IT access granted
- [ ] Email and communication tools set up
- [ ] Mandatory training completed
- [ ] Benefits enrollment completed
- [ ] Manager 1-on-1 scheduled
- [ ] Team introductions completed
- [ ] Workspace configured
- [ ] First project assigned

Resources:
- HR Portal: Access benefits, pay stubs, time off requests
- Learning Management System: Complete training courses
- Employee Handbook: Company policies and procedures
- IT Help Desk: Technical support and access issues

Support Contacts:
- HR General Questions: hr@company.com
- IT Support: it-support@company.com
- Benefits Questions: benefits@company.com
- Payroll: payroll@company.com

Your manager and HR are here to support you throughout your onboarding journey. Don't hesitate to ask questions!

Onboarding Timeline Details:

Week 2-4:
- Complete role-specific training modules
- Begin working on initial projects with guidance
- Attend team meetings and department standups
- Set up regular 1-on-1 meetings with manager
- Complete any required certifications
- Shadow experienced team members
- Begin contributing to team projects

Month 2-3:
- Take on independent project work
- Attend company-wide meetings and events
- Complete 60-day check-in with manager
- Provide feedback on onboarding experience
- Continue skill development
- Build relationships with colleagues
- Understand team dynamics and workflows

Month 4-6:
- Fully integrated into team
- Working independently on projects
- Contributing to team goals
- 90-day performance review
- Goal setting for remainder of year
- Identify areas for growth
- Begin mentoring new employees (if applicable)

Onboarding Success Metrics:
- Completion of all required training
- Positive feedback from manager
- Integration with team
- Understanding of role and responsibilities
- Comfort with tools and systems
- Knowledge of company culture

Common Onboarding Challenges:
- Information overload: Take notes and ask questions
- Feeling overwhelmed: Communicate with manager
- Technical difficulties: Contact IT support immediately
- Cultural adjustment: Participate in company events
- Role clarity: Schedule time with manager

Onboarding Resources:
- Employee handbook (digital and print)
- Internal wiki and documentation
- Training portal with video tutorials
- HR contact information
- IT support portal
- Mentor or buddy assignment

Post-Onboarding Support:
- Regular check-ins with manager
- Access to HR for questions
- Ongoing training opportunities
- Performance feedback
- Career development discussions
- Continuous improvement process
