Recruitment and Hiring Policy

Our recruitment process is designed to attract, identify, and hire the best talent while ensuring fairness and compliance.

Job Posting:
- All positions are posted internally for at least 5 business days
- External postings on job boards and company website
- Postings include job description, requirements, and salary range
- Equal opportunity employer - all qualified applicants considered

Application Process:
- Applications accepted through company career portal
- Resume and cover letter required
- Some positions may require portfolio or work samples
- Applications reviewed within 2 weeks of posting close

Interview Process:
1. Initial Screening (Phone/Video):
   - 30-minute call with recruiter
   - Review of qualifications and experience
   - Discussion of role and company culture
   - Salary expectations discussed

2. Technical/Functional Assessment:
   - Skills assessment relevant to role
   - May include coding test, writing sample, or case study
   - Completed within 1 week of initial screening

3. Interview Rounds:
   - Hiring manager interview (1 hour)
   - Team member interviews (2-3 people, 45 minutes each)
   - Final round with department head (30 minutes)
   - All interviews conducted within 2 weeks

4. Reference Checks:
   - Minimum 2 professional references required
   - References contacted after final interview
   - Background check initiated upon offer acceptance

Offer Process:
- Verbal offer extended by hiring manager
- Written offer letter sent within 24 hours
- Offer includes: salary, benefits summary, start date
- Candidates have 5 business days to respond
- Negotiation may occur within company guidelines

Onboarding:
- Start date typically 2-4 weeks after offer acceptance
- Background check and drug screening must be completed
- All required documentation submitted before start date

Internal Transfers:
- Employees can apply for internal positions
- Must notify current manager before applying
- Internal candidates given priority consideration
- Transfer requires approval from both managers

Referral Program:
- Employee referral bonus: $2,000 for successful hires
- Referrals must be submitted through company portal
- Bonus paid after new hire completes 90 days
- Referral must be hired for eligible positions

Diversity and Inclusion:
- Committed to building diverse teams
- Unconscious bias training for all interviewers
- Structured interview process to ensure fairness
- Regular review of hiring metrics

Questions about recruitment? Contact recruiting@company.com

Recruitment Metrics and Goals:
- Time to fill: Target 30-45 days
- Quality of hire: Measured through performance reviews
- Candidate experience: Survey feedback collected
- Diversity goals: Tracked and reported quarterly
- Source effectiveness: Monitor which channels work best

Interview Best Practices:
- Prepare questions in advance
- Review candidate resume thoroughly
- Create welcoming environment
- Allow time for candidate questions
- Provide clear next steps
- Give timely feedback

Candidate Communication:
- Acknowledge receipt of application within 48 hours
- Update candidates on status regularly
- Provide feedback when possible
- Respect candidate's time
- Professional communication at all times

Background Check Process:
- Conducted by third-party vendor
- Criminal history check
- Employment verification
- Education verification
- Reference checks
- Credit check (for financial positions)
- Drug screening (if required)

Offer Negotiation:
- Initial offer based on market data
- Room for negotiation within range
- Consider total compensation package
- Benefits and perks included
- Equity or bonus discussed
- Start date flexibility

Onboarding After Offer:
- Offer letter sent within 24 hours
- Background check initiated
- Pre-employment paperwork sent
- IT equipment ordered
- Workspace prepared
- Welcome package prepared

Candidate Experience:
- Professional and respectful process
- Clear communication throughout
- Timely feedback provided
- Positive company representation
- Smooth transition to onboarding

Recruitment Technology:
- Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- Video interviewing platform
- Assessment tools
- Reference checking software
- Background check integration
- Analytics and reporting

Recruitment Team:
- Recruiters: Initial screening and coordination
- Hiring Managers: Technical assessment
- HR: Process oversight and compliance
- Executives: Final approval for senior roles
- Team Members: Cultural fit assessment
