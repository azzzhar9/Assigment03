# Workplace Conduct and Code of Ethics

## Professional Standards

All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct. This includes:

### Respect and Inclusion
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Create an inclusive environment free from discrimination
- Use inclusive language in all communications

### Communication
- Maintain professional communication in all interactions
- Respond to emails and messages in a timely manner
- Be clear, concise, and respectful in written and verbal communication
- Escalate conflicts through proper channels

### Confidentiality
- Protect confidential company information
- Maintain confidentiality of employee personal information
- Do not share proprietary information outside the company
- Follow data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)

### Conflict of Interest
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest
- Do not engage in activities that compete with company interests
- Avoid personal relationships that could create conflicts
- Report potential conflicts to HR or Legal

## Prohibited Behaviors

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

1. **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, including sexual harassment, is prohibited
2. **Discrimination**: Discrimination based on protected characteristics is illegal
3. **Retaliation**: Retaliation against those who report concerns is prohibited
4. **Violence**: Threats or acts of violence are grounds for immediate termination
5. **Substance Abuse**: Being under the influence at work is prohibited
6. **Theft**: Theft of company or employee property is prohibited
7. **Fraud**: Any form of fraud or misrepresentation is prohibited

## Reporting Concerns

### How to Report
- Speak with your manager
- Contact HR directly
- Use anonymous reporting hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- Email: ethics@company.com

### Non-Retaliation Policy
- Reports made in good faith are protected
- Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited
- All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially

## Social Media Guidelines

- Do not post confidential company information
- Be respectful when mentioning the company
- Personal social media should not reflect negatively on the company
- Follow company social media policy for official accounts

## Remote Work Conduct

When working remotely:
- Maintain professional appearance for video calls
- Ensure appropriate background and environment
- Follow all company policies regardless of location
- Maintain regular communication with team

## Consequences

Violations of the code of conduct may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Required training
- Suspension
- Termination of employment

## Questions?

Contact HR at hr@company.com or your manager for clarification on any conduct policies.

## Additional Conduct Guidelines

### Professional Appearance:
- Dress appropriately for your role and work environment
- Maintain personal hygiene
- Consider client-facing responsibilities
- Follow department-specific dress codes
- Represent company professionally

### Meeting Etiquette:
- Arrive on time or notify if running late
- Come prepared with agenda items
- Participate actively and respectfully
- Minimize distractions (phones, laptops)
- Follow up on action items
- Respect others' time and contributions

### Communication Standards:
- Respond to emails within 24 hours
- Use professional language
- Be clear and concise
- Proofread important communications
- Use appropriate communication channels
- Respect response time expectations

### Collaboration:
- Share knowledge and expertise
- Support team members
- Contribute to team goals
- Participate in team activities
- Provide constructive feedback
- Celebrate team successes

### Time Management:
- Be punctual for meetings and work
- Meet deadlines and commitments
- Communicate delays early
- Prioritize work effectively
- Balance workload appropriately
- Request help when needed

### Technology Use:
- Use company technology responsibly
- Follow IT security policies
- Protect company data
- Report technical issues promptly
- Keep software updated
- Backup important work

### Office Environment:
- Maintain clean workspace
- Respect shared spaces
- Follow office protocols
- Report maintenance issues
- Conserve resources
- Be considerate of others

### Client and Vendor Relations:
- Professional representation
- Maintain confidentiality
- Follow company policies
- Escalate issues appropriately
- Build positive relationships
- Protect company interests

### Compliance:
- Follow all company policies
- Comply with legal requirements
- Report violations
- Participate in training
- Understand regulations
- Seek guidance when uncertain

### Continuous Improvement:
- Seek feedback regularly
- Learn from mistakes
- Share best practices
- Suggest improvements
- Participate in training
