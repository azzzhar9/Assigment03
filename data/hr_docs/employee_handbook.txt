Employee Handbook - Company Policies and Procedures

Welcome to Our Company:
This handbook serves as a guide to our company policies, procedures, and expectations. It is not a contract of employment but a resource to help you understand your role and our company culture.

Company Mission and Values:
Our mission is to deliver exceptional value to our customers while creating a positive work environment for our employees. Our core values include:
- Integrity: Honest and ethical in all dealings
- Innovation: Continuous improvement and creativity
- Collaboration: Working together toward common goals
- Excellence: Striving for the highest quality
- Respect: Valuing diverse perspectives and backgrounds

Employment Status:
- At-will employment: Either party may terminate at any time
- Full-time: 40 hours per week, eligible for all benefits
- Part-time: Less than 30 hours per week, limited benefits
- Contract: Temporary or project-based employment
- Intern: Student or recent graduate positions

Work Schedule:
- Standard hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Flexible hours: Available with manager approval
- Remote work: Hybrid and fully remote options
- Overtime: Must be pre-approved, paid at 1.5x rate
- Time tracking: Required for all non-exempt employees

Dress Code:
- Business casual standard
- Client-facing roles: Business professional
