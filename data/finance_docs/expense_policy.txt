Expense Reimbursement Policy

This policy outlines the procedures for submitting and reimbursing business expenses.

Eligible Expenses:
- Business travel (airfare, hotels, rental cars)
- Meals during business travel or client meetings
- Transportation (taxis, rideshare, parking)
- Business entertainment (client meals, events)
- Professional development (conferences, training)
- Office supplies and equipment (with approval)
- Internet and phone for business use (remote workers)

Expense Limits:

Meals:
- Breakfast: $25 maximum
- Lunch: $35 maximum
- Dinner: $60 maximum
- Client entertainment: $100 per person maximum
- Alcohol: 25% of meal total, requires client present

Travel:
- Airfare: Economy class, book 14+ days in advance when possible
- Hotels: $150/night maximum (major cities: $200/night)
- Rental cars: Economy or compact size
- Ground transportation: Reasonable and necessary

Receipt Requirements:
- All expenses over $25 require receipt
- Receipts must be itemized and show date, amount, vendor
- Credit card statements not sufficient alone
- Lost receipts require explanation and manager approval
- Digital receipts accepted

Expense Submission:
1. Submit expenses within 30 days of incurring expense
2. Use company expense management system
3. Attach all receipts
4. Provide business purpose for each expense
5. Select appropriate expense category
6. Submit to manager for approval

Approval Process:
- Expenses under $100: Manager approval
- Expenses $100-$500: Manager and department head approval
- Expenses over $500: Additional CFO approval required
- Approval typically within 3 business days

Reimbursement:
- Processed within 10 business days of approval
- Paid via direct deposit to bank account on file
- Reimbursement appears in next payroll cycle
- Contact payroll for reimbursement questions

Non-Reimbursable Expenses:
- Personal expenses
- Commuting to regular workplace
- Personal entertainment
- Fines and penalties
- Expenses without business purpose
- Expenses exceeding policy limits without approval
- Cash advances (use company credit card)

Company Credit Card:
- Available for employees with frequent travel
- Requires manager approval and credit check
- Must be used for all business expenses when issued
- Personal expenses prohibited
- Monthly reconciliation required
- Lost or stolen cards must be reported immediately

Travel Policy:
- Book travel through company travel portal when possible
- Pre-approval required for all business travel
- Travel request form must include: destination, dates, purpose, estimated cost
- Manager approval required before booking
- Travel insurance provided by company

Per Diem (Alternative):
- For extended travel, per diem may be offered
- $50/day for meals and incidentals
- Receipts not required for per diem
- Must choose per diem OR actual expenses, not both

International Travel:
- Additional approvals required
- Currency conversion at time of expense
- Travel insurance mandatory
- Visa and vaccination costs reimbursable
- Follow local tax and compliance requirements

Expense Audits:
- Random audits conducted quarterly
- All expense reports subject to review
- Violations may result in disciplinary action
- Fraudulent expenses result in termination

Questions? Contact Finance at finance@company.com or expenses@company.com
