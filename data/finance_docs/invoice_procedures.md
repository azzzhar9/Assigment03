# Invoice Processing Procedures

## Vendor Invoice Submission

### How to Submit Invoices

Vendors should submit invoices through:
- **Email**: invoices@company.com
- **Portal**: vendor.company.com/invoices
- **Mail**: Accounts Payable, Company Address

### Invoice Requirements

All invoices must include:
1. **Vendor Information**:
   - Legal business name
   - Tax ID or EIN
   - Contact information
   - Payment address

2. **Invoice Details**:
   - Unique invoice number
   - Invoice date
   - Due date
   - Clear description of goods/services
   - Quantity and unit prices
   - Total amount due
   - Tax amount (if applicable)

3. **Purchase Order Reference**:
   - PO number (if required)
   - Matching line items to PO

### Invoice Processing Timeline

- **Receipt**: Invoices received and logged within 1 business day
- **Review**: Finance reviews within 3 business days
- **Approval**: Department approval within 5 business days
- **Payment**: Processed within payment terms (typically Net 30)

## Internal Invoice Processing

### Employee-Submitted Expenses

Employees submit expense reports (see Expense Policy):
1. Complete expense report in system
2. Attach receipts
3. Submit to manager
4. Manager approves
5. Finance processes payment

### Purchase Orders

For purchases requiring PO:
1. Employee creates PO request
2. Manager approves PO
3. Finance issues PO to vendor
4. Vendor delivers goods/services
5. Vendor submits invoice with PO reference
6. Finance matches invoice to PO
7. Payment processed

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- **Net 30**: Payment due 30 days from invoice date
- **Net 15**: For preferred vendors
- **Net 45**: For large contracts (negotiated)
- **Due on Receipt**: For small vendors or one-time purchases

### Early Payment Discounts
- 2/10 Net 30: 2% discount if paid within 10 days
- Take advantage when cash flow allows
- Requires Finance approval

## Approval Workflows

### Invoice Approval Limits
- **Under $1,000**: Department manager
- **$1,000 - $5,000**: Department head
- **$5,000 - $25,000**: CFO
- **Over $25,000**: CEO approval required

### Approval Process
1. Finance receives and logs invoice
2. Routes to appropriate approver
3. Approver reviews and approves/rejects
4. If rejected, reason provided to vendor
5. Approved invoices queued for payment

## Payment Methods

### Preferred Methods
1. **ACH Transfer**: Preferred for regular vendors
2. **Wire Transfer**: For large amounts or international
3. **Check**: For vendors without electronic payment
4. **Credit Card**: For small purchases or subscriptions

### Payment Schedule
- **Weekly**: Every Tuesday for approved invoices
- **Urgent**: Can be processed same-day with approval
- **International**: Additional 2-3 business days

## Dispute Resolution

### Invoice Discrepancies

If invoice doesn't match PO or expectations:
1. Finance contacts vendor
2. Vendor provides clarification
3. If unresolved, escalates to department
4. May require credit memo or revised invoice

### Payment Disputes

Vendors with payment questions:
- Contact: ap@company.com
- Include invoice number and date
- Response within 2 business days

## Vendor Management

### New Vendor Setup
1. Complete vendor registration form
2. Provide W-9 (US) or tax documentation
3. Banking information for ACH
4. Finance reviews and approves
5. Vendor added to system

### Vendor Payment Information
- Update banking information: vendors@company.com
- Change payment terms: Requires contract amendment
- Tax documentation updates: Required annually

## Reporting

### Available Reports
- **Aging Report**: Outstanding invoices by age
- **Payment Report**: Payments by vendor or period
- **Approval Status**: Pending approvals
- **Spend Analysis**: Expenses by category or department

### Access
- Finance team: Full access
- Department managers: Department-specific reports
- Executives: Summary reports

## Compliance

### Tax Requirements
- 1099 forms issued for vendors over $600/year
- Sales tax collected where applicable
- International tax compliance

### Record Keeping
- All invoices retained for 7 years
- Digital storage preferred
- Audit trail maintained
